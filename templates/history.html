History

These are the major versions:

Pre-Classic: Early unreleased versions from May 10 to May 16, 2009.

Classic: Development from May 17 to November 10, 2009.

Indev: In development from December 23, 2009, to February 23, 2010.

Infdev: Infinite development from February 27 to June 30, 2010.

Alpha: Development from June 30 to December 3, 2010.

Beta: Development from December 20, 2010, to November 11, 2011.

Release: Official release on November 18, 2011, at MINECON 2011.

Latest Version: 1.21.11 as of early 2026.

While i could write every version there are 34 version without counting prefixes and infdev and lower versions. Although i could just copy paste everything from the minecraft wiki i would still need to edit through everything.
