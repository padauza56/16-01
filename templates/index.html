MINECRAFT

Minecraft is a block game made by Notch. It is a 3D game made in java. Its a sandbox adventure game in wich you need to survive and mine to get better gear and build to protect yourself or just do it for fun. The first version was just a plain field with grass blocks and rocks bellow the and the was nothing special. But later it was sold to Jeb wich after that was developed by Mojang studios.
